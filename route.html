Step-By-Step Route

This walkthrough is written so that you can follow it on a first playthrough, but it also calls out design details worth noticing. Each section pairs a custom map with an in-game screenshot so you can confirm that you are in the right room.

If you are replaying the dungeon, look at how the route gradually spirals downward: every puzzle either drops you a floor or opens a shortcut that loops back. The Deku Tree never really wastes your time – it just makes you feel like you are lost.

Floor 1 – Learning the Basics

Floor 1 is basically a lobby. Grab the map from the side room, then climb the vines and cross the platforms until you reach the door to Floor 2. Along the way, the game quietly teaches you how far you can safely drop, how Deku Babas attack, and that the web in the center is important even though you cannot break it yet.

Floor 2 – Slingshot & Verticality

Once you have the Slingshot, the dungeon opens up. On the way back from the item room, practice aiming at skulltulas and switches. The Deku Tree deliberately places enemies on walls and ceilings so that you are forced to think in 3D rather than only in front of you.

Floor 3 – Puzzles and Vertical Loops

Floor 3 exists mostly to make you comfortable with falling on purpose. Once you light torches and clear Skulltulas, the correct move is to take a running jump into the central web and crash through to the basement. It is a memorable moment because the dungeon has been teasing that web since you walked through the door.

Basement Levels – Torches, Codes, and Queen Gohma

The basement ties every mechanic together: burning webs with torches, timing jumps over water, shooting an eye switch, and entering a boss room that lives above your head instead of straight ahead. Queen Gohma herself is simple, but she’s designed to reward everything you have learned about watching the ceiling and reacting to color cues in her eye.
